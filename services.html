Services

Pawsome Pet Taxi can take you and your pet to and from your chosen destination.

Vet Appointments

I can collect and take you and your pet to your vet appointment. I am happy to assist whilst at the vets in any way necessary and will then take you both home. Alternatively, if you are unable to attend the appointment, I am more than happy to collect your pet and take them to the vets on your behalf. As a veterinary nurse, I am very familiar with all aspects of veterinary work so I can hopefully take the stress out of your vet visit.

Kennel/Cattery

Eliminate the hassle of taking your pet to the kennels or cattery. I offer safe collection and transportation to your chosen boarding facility, and I'll bring them back home once you return from your holiday.

Groomers

I can collect your pet and take them to your chosen groomer. Once they have finished being pampered, I will arrange to collect them and bring them back home.
