Our services

Pawsome Pet Taxi can take you and your pet to and from your chosen destination.

Vet Appointments

I can collect and take you to your Vet appointment. I am happy to assist whilst at the Vets in any way I can and will then take you home.

Alternatively, if you are unable to attend the appointment, I am more than happy to collect your pet and take to the Vets on your behalf.

As a Veterinary Nurse I am very familiar with all aspects of Veterinary work so I can hopefully take the stress out of your Vet visit.

Kennel/Cattery

Take the stress out of taking your Pet to the Kennels or Cattery. I can collect and transport safely to your chosen Boarding Kennels/Cattery. Once you're back from your Holiday I will bring them home again too.

Groomers

I am able to collect your Pet and take to your chosen Groomers. Once they have finished being pampered I can arrange to collect and bring back home.
